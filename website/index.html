Welcome to Rollerski Ireland

What is it?

Rollerskiing, just like cross-country skiing on which it is based, is great fun. You work for the uphills, and enjoy an exhilarating downhill – a sport, a low-impact way to get fit and lose weight, a good laugh. It’s also good cross-training for other sports and is the perfect preparation for cross-country skiing.

Who does it?

Elite nordic racers and Olympic hopefuls train on rollerskis during the offseason to prepare for their transition to snow. But also us mortals who are recreational skiers, occasional citizen racers or for us missing the snow during the summer time!
